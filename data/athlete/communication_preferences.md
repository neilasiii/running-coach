# Communication Preferences

## Detail Level: BRIEF

**Options:**
- `BRIEF`: Concise responses with just the essential information (schedule, key paces, quick adjustments)
- `STANDARD`: Balanced detail with context and rationale
- `DETAILED`: Full explanations including physiological reasoning, alternatives, and comprehensive context

**Current Setting: BRIEF**

---

## What Each Level Provides

### BRIEF Mode
- Workout prescriptions in compact format
- Just the schedule (time, intensity, paces)
- Minimal explanatory text
- Quick yes/no answers when possible
- Example:
  ```
  Tomorrow: 45 min E (10:00-11:10 pace)
  Tuesday: Threshold - 15 min E warmup, 3x10 min T (8:35 pace) w/ 2 min jog, 10 min E cooldown
  ```

### STANDARD Mode (Default)
- Workout prescriptions with brief context
- Short rationale for recommendations
- Recovery considerations mentioned
- Modification options when relevant
- Example:
  ```
  Tomorrow: 45 min E (10:00-11:10 pace) - recovery from yesterday's workout

  Tuesday: Threshold Workout
  - Warmup: 15 min E
  - Main: 3 × 10 min T (8:35) with 2 min easy jog
  - Cooldown: 10 min E
  - Purpose: Lactate threshold development
  ```

### DETAILED Mode
- Full workout prescriptions with comprehensive explanations
- Physiological reasoning for all recommendations
- Multiple modification options (conservative/moderate/full)
- Recovery protocols and coordination with other training
- Environmental/scheduling considerations
- Example:
  ```
  Tomorrow: 45 min E (10:00-11:10 pace)

  This easy run supports recovery from yesterday's threshold session. Keep HR below 145 bpm.
  The conversational pace promotes blood flow for recovery while minimizing neuromuscular fatigue.

  Tuesday: Threshold Workout
  - Warmup: 15 min E + 4 strides
  - Main: 3 × 10 min T (8:35 pace) with 2 min easy jog recovery
  - Cooldown: 10 min E
  - Purpose: Improve lactate clearance capacity and raise sustainable pace threshold

  This workout aligns with your Phase 2 (Early Quality) training. The 10-minute intervals
  provide sufficient stimulus without excessive fatigue. Recovery jogs allow HR to drop
  to 130-140 before the next rep.

  Modification options if recovery is compromised:
  - Conservative: 2 × 12 min T (same purpose, less volume)
  - Moderate: 3 × 8 min T (maintain reps, reduce duration)
  - Full: As prescribed (only if sleep >7 hrs and RHR normal)
  ```

---

## How to Change Detail Level

You can ask the coach to switch modes at any time:
- "Switch to brief mode"
- "Give me detailed explanations for this session"
- "Use standard detail level going forward"

Or edit this file directly and change the setting at the top.
