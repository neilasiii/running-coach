## Race Priority Definitions

**A-Race:** Primary goal race. Peak fitness, full taper, maximum effort. All training builds toward this.

**B-Race:** Important race but not peak priority. Abbreviated taper (3-7 days), strong effort, used to gauge fitness or as prep race.

**C-Race:** Lower priority race. Minimal taper (2-3 days easy running), used as hard workout or race-pace practice. Should not compromise key training.

**Training Race:** Part of a long run or quality workout. Race effort but no taper. Used for race simulation and fueling practice.

**Shakeout Race:** Low-stakes tune-up race. Easy/moderate effort, primarily for logistics practice or fun.
