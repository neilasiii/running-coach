# Post-Marathon Recovery Plan (2 Weeks)

**Week 1: Post-Marathon Recovery**

*   **Monday, November 24:** Rest
*   **Tuesday, November 25:** 20-30 min E (10:00-11:10 pace)
*   **Wednesday, November 26:** Rest
*   **Thursday, November 27:** 20-30 min E (10:00-11:10 pace)
*   **Friday, November 28:** Rest
*   **Saturday, November 29:** 30-40 min E (10:00-11:10 pace)
*   **Sunday, November 30:** Rest or light mobility

**Week 2: Continued Recovery / Gentle Reintroduction**

*   **Monday, December 1:** Rest
*   **Tuesday, December 2:** 30-40 min E (10:00-11:10 pace)
*   **Wednesday, December 3:** Rest
*   **Thursday, December 4:** 30-40 min E (10:00-11:10 pace)
*   **Friday, December 5:** Rest
*   **Saturday, December 6:** 40-50 min E (10:00-11:10 pace)
*   **Sunday, December 7:** Rest or light mobility

This plan prioritizes rest and very easy, short runs to promote recovery. Listen to your body and feel free to take extra rest days if needed.
